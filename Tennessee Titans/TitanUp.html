Tennessee Titans

What is TitanUP?

"TitanUP" is more than just a slogan—it's a rallying cry that embodies the spirit, unity, and resilience of the Tennessee Titans and their fans. Whether it's on the field during a hard-fought game or in the stands cheering their team, "TitanUP" represents the grit and determination that defines the Titans community. It's a call to action, urging fans to rise up, support the team, and stand strong together through every victory and challenge.

The "TitanUP" movement has grown into a symbol of pride for the Titans fan base, uniting supporters in their passion for the team. From the electrifying atmosphere at Nissan Stadium to the vibrant fan communities online, "TitanUP" is a way for fans to show their unwavering loyalty and love for the team. Whether you're a lifelong fan or new to the Titans family, "TitanUP" means standing tall, proud, and ready for the battle ahead.

Game Day Traditions

Game days for the Tennessee Titans are filled with excitement, passion, and time-honored traditions that bring fans together to celebrate their team. Whether you're at Nissan Stadium or cheering from home, these traditions help create an unforgettable game day experience.

  • The "Titan Walk": Before each home game, fans line up to watch the players make their way into the stadium, cheering them on as they arrive in their game-day gear. This close interaction fuels the energy between the team and its loyal supporters.
  • "Code Blue": Once a season, fans unite by wearing all blue to create a sea of Titans pride in the stands. It’s a powerful display of team spirit, showing that the entire fan base is behind the team.
  • "Fire the Cannon": Every time the Titans score, a celebratory cannon is fired at Nissan Stadium, adding an explosive element to touchdowns and key plays. The sound of the cannon is a signal to fans that the Titans are dominating.
  • Tailgating: Tailgating at Nissan Stadium is a long-standing tradition, where fans gather hours before kickoff to enjoy food, drinks, and camaraderie. Barbecues, music, and Titans gear fill the parking lots, setting the stage for the excitement ahead.
  • T-Rac the Mascot: The beloved Titans mascot, T-Rac, gets the crowd pumped with his energetic antics, keeping the energy high throughout the game.

Join the Titans Community

Becoming a part of the Tennessee Titans community is about more than just being a fan—it's about joining a family that stands behind their team through every high and low. Whether you're at Nissan Stadium on game day or cheering from home, the Titans community offers countless ways to connect with fellow fans, share your passion, and celebrate the team's victories together.

  • Titans Fan Clubs: Join local or national fan clubs to connect with fellow Titans enthusiasts, attend exclusive events, and receive team news. These clubs are perfect for both lifelong fans and newcomers looking to get involved.
  • Social Media and Forums: Follow the Titans on social media platforms like Twitter, Instagram, and Facebook for the latest updates, behind-the-scenes content, and fan interactions. Participate in fan forums and discussion groups to share your thoughts, predictions, and opinions on all things Titans.
  • Game Day Events: Aside from the game itself, Titans game day features a variety of events like tailgating parties, meet-and-greets with alumni players, and live music performances to enhance the experience.
  • Titans Foundation and Community Outreach: The Titans are deeply involved in the Nashville community through charitable efforts and outreach programs. By supporting the Titans Foundation, fans can contribute to making a difference in the community, from supporting youth sports to helping families in need.

By joining the Titans community, you’re not just supporting a team—you’re becoming part of a culture that celebrates resilience, unity, and passion for football. It's time to "TitanUP" and be a part of something bigger!

Show Your Support

Support the Tennessee Titans in style and pride! Whether you're cheering from the stands, at home, or out in the community, showing your support means wearing your team colors and embracing the Titans spirit. Here are some great ways to get involved and display your loyalty:

  • Titans Gear: Deck yourself out in the latest Titans jerseys, hats, and apparel. Visit the official Titans online store or stop by the stadium on game day to grab merchandise that proudly represents the team. Wearing your Titans gear is the perfect way to show off your team pride wherever you go.
  • Tailgate in Style: Bring your passion to game day with Titans-branded gear for tailgating. From grills and coolers to flags and chairs, make your tailgating experience stand out while supporting your team.
  • Social Media Engagement: Share your Titans love on social media. Post pictures of your game-day outfits, tailgating setup, or even your home decked out in Titans decor. Use the hashtag #TitanUP and join the online conversation with fellow fans and the team.
  • Titans License Plates and Decals: Show your support every time you're on the road with official Titans license plates, decals, and car flags. It’s a simple way to represent your team year-round.
  • Support the Titans Foundation: Your support can extend beyond the field. Contribute to the Titans Foundation to help with community efforts, from youth sports initiatives to educational programs and charitable outreach.

By showing your support, you’re not just cheering for the Titans—you’re celebrating a way of life and representing the team's pride, commitment, and dedication. So, grab your gear, wave your flag, and be proud to “TitanUP”!
